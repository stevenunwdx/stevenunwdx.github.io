Academic Biography

I am a research mathematician at The University of Iowa . My primary research area is stochastic analysis applied to mathematical finance. I currently work under the supervision of Palle E.T. Jorgensen.

Personal Biography

I am an American-born Chinese. My family is originally from the southern Chinese province of Fujian, speaking the southern Min dialect. I grew up living in the east coast of the United States in a suburb of New York City.
